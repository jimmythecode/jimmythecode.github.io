I helped to create this full-stack ReactJS application with a team during my bootcamp in Haifa, Israel.

The app was eventually further developed and is now used by Israeli citizens to organise their blood donations.

I primarily worked with Mapbox to create an interactive map that allows users to find nearby donation locations and plan their trip. More details can be seen on this presentation video.

Clicking the gif image will take you to an earlier working version of the app (however it doesn't have access to the blood donation servers so has very limited functionality).

Does Anyone Else?

A quick codesandbox demo I made to try out typescript. The concept is to ask/answer questions and learn new information about personality traits. I thought it could be a fun way for people with ADHD to find out about themselves, others, and the latest understanding of ADHD.

For the comments section I managed to use recursion to generate nested comments (I'm still not quite sure how it works but it does!)
